Privacy Policy for Proclip Studio

Last updated: April 2026

Proclip Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: We use the identity permission to allow you to log in via Google. We only receive your basic profile information (such as email address) to verify your premium access status. We do not store your passwords.
  • Link Data: Our extension collects publicly visible URLs of videos from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook while you browse, but only when you explicitly activate the collection process. This data is processed locally on your device and only sent to a Google Form when you actively initiate the submission.

2. Permissions We Request

  • Identity: Required to authenticate users and manage access to premium features securely.
  • Storage: Used to save your local settings, such as which platforms are enabled for link collection.
  • Content Scripts: Used to read public video links on the supported platforms when you initiate the process.
  • Host Permissions: Limited only to the platforms you collect links from (YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram) and Google Forms (for you to submit your collected links).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of collecting video links.
  • To verify your premium access status for continued use of the extension.
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do NOT track your overall browsing history. The extension only interacts with the specific supported pages when you command it to.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (Firebase Authentication) to secure your login status. The collected links are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently on any external servers managed by us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page or at our developer contact email.
